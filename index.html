About Us

Vividha Welfare Foundation

Founded in 2020 by Snehil Pallav Sinha, Vividha Welfare Foundation is a registered trust dedicated to advancing science education and communication, promoting rural development, and empowering young learners across India. Since its inception, Vividha has actively worked to bridge gaps in experiential learning, scientific inquiry, and community engagement.

Vividha played a key role in nurturing young innovators through its participation in the Indian Young Inventors Challenge in 2021 and 2022, where our teams supported student ideation, project development, and science communication. These experiences strengthened our commitment to fostering a culture of critical thinking and creativity among students.

Throughout its journey, Vividha has worked hand in hand with Prasansa Foundation, a grassroots non-governmental trust that focuses on education, rural development, women's empowerment, and science & technology-related interventions in local communities. Drawing on shared values of inclusivity, sustainable development, and scientific outreach, our collaboration amplifies impact across educational and community platforms.

Driven by compassion and innovation, Vividha continues to inspire young minds, support educators, and contribute to equitable access to quality science learning.
